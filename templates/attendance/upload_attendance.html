CSV Format Requirements
Required Headers (in order):
  • EP Number
  • Name
  • Company Name
  • Plant
  • Department
  • Trade
  • Skill
  • Shift
  • Date (DD-MM-YYYY)
  • IN1 (HH:MM)
  • OUT1 (HH:MM)
  • IN2 (HH:MM)
  • OUT2 (HH:MM)
  • IN3 (HH:MM)
  • OUT3 (HH:MM)
  • Hours Worked (H:MM or decimal)
  • Overtime (H:MM or decimal)
  • Status (P/A/-0.5/-1)
Notes:
  • New EP numbers will auto-create employee accounts
  • Time format: 24-hour (08:30, 17:45) or next day (02:30 (N))
  • Date format: DD-MM-YYYY (09-09-2025)
  • Hours format: H:MM (8:30) or decimal (8.5)
  • Empty time fields are allowed
  • Status: P=Present, A=Absent, -0.5=Half Day, -1=Full Deduction
